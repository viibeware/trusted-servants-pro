Compliance module

Toggle the banner on or off site-wide. When off, no banner is shown and no consent cookie is set.
Enable cookie compliance banner
Renders a privacy banner on the public site until each visitor has made a choice. Their choice is remembered in their browser for {{ site.cookie_compliance_remember_days or 365 }} day{{ '' if (site.cookie_compliance_remember_days or 365) == 1 else 's' }}.
Auto-adapt to visitor region
When on, EU/UK visitors get the stricter GDPR opt-in flow and California visitors get a CCPA "do not sell or share" choice, regardless of the mode you pick below. Visitors outside those regions see your configured mode. Your chosen mode is the floor — auto can only escalate, never relax.

Prompt mode

How forcefully the banner asks for consent. Strict is required to meet GDPR.
