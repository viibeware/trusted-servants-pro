Utility bar

A slim row built into every page's header for status text, secondary links, social icons, or call-to-action buttons. Anything you add to the left or right shows up at the matching edge. Group related items into a container so they render as one unit on the public site and become a single mobile swipe target.

    Mobile collapsed default

    On narrow viewports the bar collapses to a horizontal swipe strip — visitors swipe left or right to reveal items that don't fit. Pick which item, container, or whole side shows by default. The Live Meetings Bar always pins above the strip when it's active.

  • Live Meetings Bar

    When on, the utility bar turns yellow as soon as an online or hybrid meeting's open time is reached, and shows the meeting name plus a Join Zoom Meeting button until the meeting ends (or the next live meeting starts).
